Service Account gives -> Identity for pod/workload.

SA = Who are you?
Role = What can you do?
Binding = Who gets what?

Diff:
ServiceAccount -> Identity
Role -> Permission

#Service Account only gives identity.
#Permission comes from:
- Role + RoleBinding
- ClusterRole + ClusterRoleBinding


Created by using cmd:
kubectl create role dev-role -n dev --verb=get,list,watch --resource=pods -o yaml > role.yaml
kubectl create rolebinding dev-rolebinding -n dev --role=dev-role --serviceaccount=dev:dev-sa -o yaml > rolebinding.yaml


Testing:(yes/no)
