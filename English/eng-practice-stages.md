Since you're learning English to speak confidently as a Junior DevOps Engineer, don't start with grammar-heavy topics. Focus on **sentence-building topics** that you can use every day.

# 🎯 Stage 1: Foundation (Learn to Make Sentences)

Master these first:

## 1. Pronouns

* I, You, We, They, He, She, It

Examples:

* I am Rahul.
* She is working.
* They are playing.

---

## 2. Be Verbs

* am
* is
* are
* was
* were

Examples:

* I am happy.
* He is busy.
* We are learning English.

---

## 3. Daily Action Verbs (Most Important)

Learn 50 common verbs:

| Verb  | Example                      |
| ----- | ---------------------------- |
| Eat   | I eat breakfast.             |
| Go    | I go to office.              |
| Come  | He comes late.               |
| Work  | I work as a DevOps Engineer. |
| Learn | I learn Kubernetes.          |
| Watch | I watch YouTube videos.      |
| Read  | I read books.                |
| Write | I write notes.               |
| Play  | They play cricket.           |
| Sleep | I sleep at 11 PM.            |

Goal:

* Make 5 sentences daily using one verb.

---

## 4. Subject + Verb + Object Pattern

Most English sentences follow:

```text
Subject + Verb + Object
```

Examples:

* I eat rice.
* She reads books.
* We deploy applications.
* They play football.

Practice 20 sentences daily.

---

## 5. WH Questions

* What
* Why
* When
* Where
* Who
* Which
* How

Examples:

* What are you doing?
* Where do you live?
* Why are you late?
* How do you deploy applications?

---

## 6. Yes/No Questions

Examples:

* Do you work?
* Are you busy?
* Did you eat?
* Have you finished?

---

# 🎯 Stage 3: Workplace English

Since you're working in DevOps, practice these:

## Daily Standup

```text
Yesterday I worked on Kubernetes deployment.

Today I will work on Jenkins pipeline.

Currently I have no blockers.
```

---

## Asking Questions

```text
Can you help me?

Could you explain this issue?

Can you share the logs?

Can you check the deployment?
```

---

## Giving Updates

```text
The deployment is completed.

The issue has been resolved.

The service is running fine.

The pipeline failed due to a configuration issue.
```

# 🎯 Stage 4: Sentence Building Drills

Every day pick one pattern and make 20 sentences.

### Pattern 1

```text
I am ______
```

Examples:

* I am tired.
* I am learning English.
* I am a DevOps Engineer.

---

### Pattern 2

```text
I like ______
```

Examples:

* I like cricket.
* I like coding.
* I like coffee.

---

### Pattern 3

```text
I want to ______
```

Examples:

* I want to learn Kubernetes.
* I want to improve my English.
* I want to switch jobs.

---

### Pattern 4

```text
I can ______
```

Examples:

* I can code.
* I can deploy applications.
* I can solve problems.
