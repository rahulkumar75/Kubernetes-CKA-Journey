Pick **one verb** and make sentences in different tenses.

```text

Present Perfect:
I have gone to the office.

Past:
I went to the office.

Future:
I will go to the office.
```

# 🚀 The Exercise That Will Help You Most

Every night, answer these 5 questions in English:

```text
1. What did I do today?

2. What am I doing these days?

3. What did I learn today?

4. What challenge did I face today?

5. What will I do tomorrow?
```

This forces you to use:

* Past tense
* Present tense
* Present continuous
* Present perfect
* Future tense

every day in real situations.

# Memory Formula for Speaking

```text
Past     → Yesterday I...

Present  → I...

Now      → I am...

Experience → I have...

Future   → I will...
```

Whenever you're stuck while speaking, think:

```text
Yesterday?  → Past

Now?        → Present Continuous

Habit?      → Present Simple

Completed?  → Present Perfect

Tomorrow?   → Future
```
